To make the relevance feedback understandable, you can follow these steps:

star    Explain Result
1. Click on    assessment    Explain Result
2. Pop-up shows a Scatterplot represntation of how the relevance feedback has effected the query vector. The revised query vector moves closer to the relevant documents.
 star    Key-Phrase Explanation
1. Click on    assessment    Key-Phrase explanation
2. An explanation will be shown of which keywords from the selected relevant documents are matching with the current documents.
 star    Context explanation
1. Click on    assessment    Context explanation
2. A bar-chart representation shows how many time the key-phrases from the current documents appear wthin 5-word-context of the key-phrase from the selected document
 star    Highlight Context and Key-phrase
Key-phrases that are present in the 5-word-vicinity are highlighted.
